Our History

In 2003 Jerad Tjaden started a job he never dreamed would turn into a full time career. After years of installing, sanding and finishing hardwood floors for other businesses, he decided it was time to start his own company in 2014, Hardwood Hero.

As owner and operator of Hardwood Hero, Jerad firmly believes in using the highest quality of products for your hardwood floors from start to finish. Hardwood Hero takes pride in being a small local business, leaving the overhead low, and passing those savings on to the homeowner.

As a Hardwood Hero customer, you will appreciate Jerad's organization, attention to detail, reliability, and passion for his industry.
